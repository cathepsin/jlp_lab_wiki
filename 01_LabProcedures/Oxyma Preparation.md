# Oxyma Preparation
## Summary

Preparation of Oxyma solution used especially for [[Solid Phase Peptide Synthesis - OP Microwave]]. This prepared solution is usually good for 24 hours

## Materials
- An appropriately sized vessel for scale

## Reagents
- [[Oxyma]]*
- [[NMP]]*
\* *See notes section for reagent amounts*
## Procedure
1. Combine [[Oxyma]] and [[NMP]] in a labeled vessel
2. Vortex mixture until totally homogenous

- [[DIC Preparation]]
