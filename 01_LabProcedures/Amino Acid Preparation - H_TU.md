# Amino Acid Preparation (Room Temperature)
## Summary
Preparation of an amino acid using HCTU. Most commonly used for [[Solid Phase Peptide Synthesis - RT]].

## Materials
- 1 small dram vial
## Reagents
- FMOC-AA-OH Amino acid*
- [[H_TU Reagent Preparation|H_TU Coupling solution]]*
- [[DIEA]]*
\* *See notes for reagent amounts*

## Procedure
1. Weigh out appropriate amount of FMOC-AA-OH amino acid powder in a dram vial
2. Dissolve FMOC-AA-OH amino acid powder in [[H_TU Reagent Preparation|H_TU coupling solution]]. Vortex if needed
3. After fully dissolved, add DIEA to the dram vial. For most amino acids, the solution will tend to turn yellow. Vortex briefly to homogenize

## Notes
Amino acid solutions are mostly stable. Despite this, it is not advised that a solution be prepared more than an hour or two before being used.
