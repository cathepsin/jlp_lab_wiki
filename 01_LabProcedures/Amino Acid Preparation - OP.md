# Amino Acid Preparation (One Pot)
## Summary
Preparation of an amino acid using HCTU. Most commonly used for [[Solid Phase Peptide Synthesis - RT]].

## Materials
- 1 small dram vial
## Reagents
- FMOC-AA-OH Amino acid*
- [[Oxyma Preparation|Oxyma coupling solution]]*
- [[DIC Preparation|DIC coupling solution]]*
\* *See notes for reagent amounts*

## Procedure
1. Weigh out appropriate amount of FMOC-AA-OH amino acid powder in a dram vial
2. Dissolve FMOC-AA-OH amino acid powder in [[Oxyma Preparation|oxyma]] and [[DIC Preparation|DIC]] coupling solutions. Vortex if needed.

- [[DIC Preparation]]
- [[H_TU Reagent Preparation]]

## Notes

### Oxyma and DIC Solutions
The [[Oxyma Preparation|oxyma]] and [[DIC Preparation|DIC]] coupling solutions should be used in equal amounts when preparing your amino acid. In general, *0.625ml* should be used for every 25µl of scale. Below is a table for common scales:

| Target Scale | Oxyma Solution | DIC Solution |
| ------------ | -------------- | ------------ |
| 25µl         | 0.625ml        | 0.625ml      |
| 50µl         | 1.250ml        | 1.250ml      |
| 75µl         | 1.875ml        | 1.875ml      |
| 100µl        | 2.500ml        | 2.500ml      |
| 150µl        | 3.750ml        | 3.750ml      |
| 200µl        | 5.000ml        | 5.000ml      |
| 250µl        | 6.250ml        | 6.250ml      |
