# H\*TU Coupling Solution Preparation
## Summary
Preparation of coupling H\*TU coupling solutions. Most commonly used for [[Solid Phase Peptide Synthesis - RT]]
## Materials
- Amber storage container or other appropriate light-protective container
## Reagents
- [[HBTU]], [[HCTU]], or [[HATU]]
- [[NMP|NMP]]
- [[HOBt]]
\* *See notes for reagent amounts*
## Procedure
1. In your storage container, add [[NMP]] and [[HOBt]]
2. Next, add [[HBTU]], [[HCTU]], or [[HATU]]
3. Stir until well dissolved and homogenous
